    • Changelog

    Changes in version 3.41

    Arreglo
    Drivers will recognize tip triggers again (instead of ignoring them after the 3rd, 4th drive-through. Includes performance improvements in finding tip triggers.
    Nuevo
    Trucks with own fruit transport capabilities now unload correctly.
    Nuevo
    Field work mode: support for the Ursus Z-586 round bale wrapper by Giants.
    Cambio
    If the ESLimiter, ThreshingCounter or Odometer huds are positioned on top/underneath the Courseplay hud, they will now be hidden when the Courseplay hud is open.
    Arreglo
    Combi mode: drivers will no longer swerve when unloading a combine.
    Arreglo
    Support for nonarticulated wheel loaders: they will no longer swerve.

    Changes in version 3.40

    Arreglo
    Fertilizer/seeding mode: fixed an error where too many waiting points (3 or more) would crash the game. (Still, two waiting points is the way to go!)
    Arreglo
    Combi mode: After turning from the chopper, the driver won't crash into the next driver in the chain anymore.
    Arreglo
    Combine drives to trailer and unloads: Game won't crash anymore when the unloading course is being driven before having activated the AI helper.
    Arreglo
    Fixed error in tippers.lua, where the game would crash if an implement didn't have a valid name.
    Arreglo
    Fixed error where hud opening setting (keyboard vs. mouse) wouldn't be saved and loaded correctly.

    Note: With existing savegames, this can lead to the hud not opening with the mouse. Please see the FAQ for further information ("After loading my old savegame, I can't open the Courseplay hud anymore").
    Cambio
    Combi and overload modes: Drivers should always have the trailer directly under the pipe, instead of too far ahead or behind (regarding the vertical offset).
    Nuevo

    Courseplay en Español

    In Spanish game versions, the Courseplay hud is shown in Spanish. Muchas gracias a JFMtb por hacerlo.
    Nuevo

    Courseplay in italiano

    In Italian game versions, the Courseplay hud is shown in Italian. Molte grazie a JD7530.
    Cambio
    Fieldwork mode: Balers are now activated at the first waiting point, and deactivated at the second one.
    Cambio
    At the end of the lane, combines won't wait for the driver anymore if said driver is more than 100 metres away.
    Arreglo
    On the course management page, navigation arrows are displayed correctly again.
    Nuevo
    Fieldwork mode: support for Universal Bale Trailer (UBT)
    Nuevo
    Fertilizer mode: In the refilling course, an additional waiting point can be set. At this point, the manure spreader will wait until it's filled 100%, then continue.
    Nuevo

    New course generation

    • Choice of starting corner and starting direction
    • Choice of headland lane (before or after)
    • Option to activate returning to the starting point (e.g. if the the unloading/refilling course starts and ends at the field course's starting point)
    • No more "X" courses (where all lanes would go through the field's center point)
    • Turning maneuver at the end of the lane, incl. driving backwards (with supported tools), lifting, deactiving, etc.
    • Choice for automatic or manual ridge markers
    Arreglo
    Combining an unsaved course with a saved one won't throw an error anymore.
    Nuevo

    Courseplay v českém

    In Czech game versions, the Courseplay hud is shown in Czech. Děkuji, Albi83.
    Nuevo
    Fertilizer mode now supports multiple tools simultaneously. For example, a sprayer in the back, and a spray tank in the front.
    Cambio
    The work width's steps are now 0.5m/2m when above 10m.
    Nuevo
    Fieldwork mode: Support for the Krone BigM
    Nuevo

    Copy course

    Copy another vehicle's current course. Also works with combined or unsaved courses.
    Arreglo
    Combi mode: when the combine/chopper is driven manually, the unloading driver won't drive into the fruit anymore when turning.
    Nuevo
    Combi mode: when the combine/chopper is driven manually, you need to tell the tractor driver that you're starting (and finishing) the turning maneuver, in order for him to get out of the way. This can be found on the very first page ("Combine Controls"): Turning maneuver" - "Start"/"Finish".
    Nuevo
    Opening and closing trailer covers: more supported trailers like the Hobein 18t (Fuqsbow) and Giants' default trailers
    Arreglo
    Liquid manure transfer: works as it should again (only with the Abbey Nurse Tanker for now).
    Nuevo
    Updated hud, new buttons for quickly switching the mode and choosing the page, new click sound
    Arreglo
    Improved fruit and field recognition with potatoes and sugar beets
    Arreglo

    Improved procedures for combine in field work mode

    • Combine interrupts work during rain
    • When being unloaded, combine continues work when its fill level is at 80%
    • At the lane end, the combine will initiate its turning maneuver as soon as the unloading driver has reached a safe distance
    Nuevo
    Support for attached combines (e.g. Grimme Rootster, Grimme SE75) except attachable choppers
    Nuevo

    New mode: fill and empty shovel

    • The shovel is filled in a designated area (e.g. BGA silo), reverses and is emptied at a BGA bunker or into a trailer.
    • The four shovel positions have to be set ("recorded") at the beginning: loading, transport, pre-unloading, unloading positions
    Arreglo
    Improved driving characteristics for center-pivot steering vehicles (e.g. wheel loader, Weideman)
    Nuevo
    Support for AlternativeTipping (Xentro/SFM) and PlaceableHeapTrigger (mor2000)
    Nuevo
    Sugarbeet loaders are recognized as combines, meaning trailers can drive to and unload them automatically (Ropa EuroMaus 3 and Holmer Terra Felis by Maciusboss1 and Burner).

    Changes in version 3.30

    Arreglo
    The never ending story of improving the automatic offset - should work with all standard machinery, including attachable combines
    Nuevo

    BGA: division into 3 segments during the unloading process

    The silo is divided into three segments, and the tractor (driver) automatically decides which section to unload into, based on which is the emptiest. After a couple of trailers have been unloaded, this should guarantee an even distribution in the silo
    Nuevo

    Speeds

    • The tractors should now adapt better to the combine's/chopper's speed during unloading (combi mode)
    • The speed settings now support the ESLimiter (version 3 and up) – quite beautifully if we may say so
    • new speed setting "Unloading (BGA)" for, surprise surprise, the speed during unloading in the BGA silos
    • Speed adaption to traffic flow
    Arreglo
    Vast improvements with the turn maneuvers during unloading the chopper (combi mode). Tractors crashing into the chopper should never happen at all anymore. Now even the turn radius gets a second life, it will be important to set it fitting to the current tractor
    Cambio
    new turn maneuver for unloading a combine (combi and overloader mode)
    Arreglo
    Small improvements for the forced driver side during unloading the chopper (combi mode)
    Arreglo
    The driver should now stop at the first waypoint in "Driver" mode
    Arreglo
    Automatic refueling should work with most machines again
    Arreglo
    The course list can be scrolled through faster by using the CTRL key
    Arreglo
    improved calculation which driver should head to a calling combine (based on distance)
    Arreglo
    fixed graphics warnings (2^n), incl. the display of the yellow arrow when pausing the recording
    Arreglo
    Forage wagons will be re-activated upon resuming the field course (lowering and activating the pickup)
    Nuevo
    Display of speed in mph for English game versions
    Nuevo

    Automatic calculation of turning circle

    • based on wheel base, wheel track, steering angle and amount of trailers
    • set from 'auto' to 'manual' with +/-
    • set from 'manual' to 'auto' by setting it to 0 (zero)
    Arreglo
    Three sided tippers (unloading left or right) are able again to unload (and continue to work afterwards)
    Nuevo

    Seeding mode

    • "Fertilizer" mode has become "Fertilizer and seeding"
    • automatic (un)folding, raising/lowering, (de)activating
    • like fertilizers, empty seeders can be refilled automatically by driving through a seed trigger
    Nuevo
    Field work mode supports new tools: cultivators, ploughs, windrowers and tedders
    Arreglo
    Patch 1.4: Yellow arrow won't be shown during loading the map
    Nuevo
    Fertilizer mode: support for Fuchs liquid manure barrel and Tebbe HS180 manure spreader
    Arreglo
    Support for horizontal offset in fertilizer/seeding mode
    Cambio

    Completely new "Combine drives to trailer and unloads" mode

    • The combine returns and aligns with its lane way more accurately
    • Pipe is opened earlier
    • AI helper course and unloading course aren't mixed together and saved anymore
    Cambio
    Work width is shown as a red line (better visibility), including horizontal offset
    Cambio
    Course is followed 50% more accurately – waypoints are skipped less frequently

    Support for Manual Ignition
